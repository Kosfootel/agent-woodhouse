## Executive Summary

Microsoft Build 2026, held on June 2, 2026, marked a pivotal moment for Microsoft's AI and developer strategy. The conference focused heavily on agentic AI systems, quantum computing breakthroughs, and the company's comprehensive agent platform. Key announcements included the launch of Microsoft Scout (an OpenClaw-inspired personal assistant), Majorana 2 quantum chip, Microsoft Discovery for agentic workflows, and Project Solara for hardware innovation. The event positioned Microsoft as a leader in the emerging agentic AI landscape while accelerating its quantum computing roadmap.

### 🔹 Quantum Computing

#### Majorana 2 Quantum Chip
- **Announcement:** Next-generation topological quantum chip
- **Key Improvement:** Qubits are 1,000 times more reliable than Majorana 1
- **Material Stack Changes:**
  - Replaced aluminum superconductor with lead
  - Updated semiconductor to indium arsenide and indium arsenide antimonide combination
- **Performance:**
  - Qubit lifetimes: Exceed 20 seconds (Majorana 1: 1-12 milliseconds)
  - Some qubit lifetimes now exceed a minute
- **Timeline:** Microsoft has cut its timeline in half and now aims for fault-tolerant quantum computer by 2029
- **Author:** Chetan Nayak, Microsoft technical fellow and corporate vice president of quantum hardware
- **Link:** https://www.theverge.com/news/940874/microsoft-majorana-2-quantum-chip-build
